iHome

One of my interests is automating daily routines, which is why I am working on home development. Not only it is cool and progressive, but it is also convenient to have your home adjust to your needs and ease your daily life. For this, I use Raspberry Pi 4 as a dedicated server with Home Assistant.

ML Sport

Are you interested in sports? If so, you may know that there are several bookmakers who accept bets on a wide range of sports. This project aims to identify inefficiencies in betting odds by leveraging machine learning algorithms to analyze statistical data. While a broker's coefficient reflects their belief in a particular outcome and takes into account current rates, our goal is to more accurately predict outcome. Ultimately, we aim to identify any distortions in bookmakers' coefficients relative to our predicted ones.

Trade4Me

Another area of interest for me is the stock market. We are currently living in a time of great intrigue as global economics undergo significant changes and potential crises loom on the horizon. There are compelling indications that the world order is shifting, and we find ourselves in the initial stages of these transformative shifts. Isn't it absolutely fascinating? Global macro trends provide us with invaluable insights into the potential future. My objective is to diligently monitor these trends and strive to interpret them with utmost accuracy. The purpose of this project is to develop practical automations that facilitate efficient market observation and enable prompt decision-making. Moreover, in the final stages, I aim to leverage machine learning to automatically execute trade decisions.
