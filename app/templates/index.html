iHome

One of my interests is automating daily routines, which is why I am working on home development. Not only it is cool and progressive, but it is also convenient to have your home adjust to your needs and ease your daily life. For this, I use Raspberry Pi 4 as a dedicated server with Home Assistant.

ML Sport

Are you interested in sports? If so, you may know that there are several bookmakers who accept bets on a wide range of sports. While a broker's coefficient reflects their belief in a particular outcome and takes into account current rates. But are they correct?

In this project I aims to identify inefficiencies in betting odds by leveraging machine learning algorithms to analyze statistical data. Ultimately, I aim to identify any distortions in bookmakers' coefficients relative to my predicted ones.

Trade4Me

We are currently living in a time of great intrigue as global economics undergo significant changes and potential crises loom on the horizon. There are compelling indications that the world order is shifting, and we find ourselves in the initial stages of these transformative shifts. Isn't it absolutely fascinating?

The purpose of this project is to develop practical automations that facilitate efficient market observation and enable prompt decision-making.
