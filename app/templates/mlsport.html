ML Sport

Are you interested in sports? If so, you may know that there are several bookmakers who accept bets on a wide range of sports. This project aims to identify inefficiencies in betting odds by leveraging machine learning algorithms to analyze statistical data. While a broker's coefficient reflects their belief in a particular outcome and takes into account current rates, our goal is to more accurately predict outcome. Ultimately, we aim to identify any distortions in bookmakers' coefficients relative to our predicted ones.
